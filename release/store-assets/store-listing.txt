Zelv Track – Routine & Habit Tracker

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Build better habits. Track projects. Shop smarter. All offline, all private.

FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max):
Zelv Track is a clean, focused productivity app designed for people who want to build strong daily routines, manage projects with real structure, and keep a smart shopping list — all in one place, all stored privately on your device.

🌅 DAILY ROUTINE TRACKER
Your day is split into time blocks — Morning, Work, Midday, Afternoon, Evening, and Night. Each block holds the habits that belong there. Tap to check them off as you go. A Good Morning card at the top shows your streak, today's progress, and the date.

Non-negotiable habits are highlighted so you never lose sight of your anchors.

📊 STATS & HEATMAP
See how consistent you've really been. A 7-day bar chart shows your daily completion percentage at a glance. The habit heatmap colours each cell by time block so you can spot patterns — are you great in the morning but dropping off in the evening?

A ranked habit list shows your most and least consistent habits so you know exactly where to focus.

🎯 PROJECTS — 3-LEVEL HIERARCHY
Real projects have real structure. Zelv Track gives you:

  Project → Task Group → Subtask

Create a project like "Home Renovation", add groups like "Planning", "Building", "Finishing", then add individual subtasks inside each. Collapse groups you're not working on. Archive projects when they're done. Add everything inline — no extra screens, no friction.

🛒 SMART SHOPPING LIST
Organise your shopping by store. Items are grouped under shops (Migros, Coop, Lidl, Aldi, Pharmacy — fully customisable). Add a brand field so you never grab the wrong product. Tap to check off items as you shop. Done items move to an "In Cart" section at the bottom.

🎤 GOOGLE ASSISTANT INTEGRATION
Control Zelv Track hands-free:
• "Hey Google, tick cold shower in Zelv Track"
• "Hey Google, add milk to shopping in Zelv Track"
• "Hey Google, mark pension transfer done in Zelv Track"
• "Hey Google, create project Home Renovation in Zelv Track"
• "Hey Google, open shopping in Zelv Track"

🔒 100% PRIVATE, 100% OFFLINE
No account. No internet required. No analytics. No ads. Everything is stored locally on your device using Hive. Uninstall the app and all data is gone — we never touched it.

🎨 BEAUTIFUL, CUSTOMISABLE DESIGN
Light and dark mode. Six accent colour presets. Block-specific colours throughout — amber for morning, green for midday, violet for afternoon, and more — so the app feels alive and visual.
