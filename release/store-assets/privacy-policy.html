Zelv Track

Privacy Policy   Last updated: June 2026

The short version

Zelv Track does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Ever.

What data the app stores

All data you enter — habits, projects, shopping lists — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive). It never leaves your phone.

Internet access

Zelv Track does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests.

Third-party services

None. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, no tracking of any kind.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/itsgurmailsingh-png/zelv-track
